Reduce Your Use Tampa Bay

Not everything can be recycled, so do more with less! Switch to reusable items and reduce your use of single-use plastics. Reducing waste saves money and helps the environment. Be part of the solution to plastic pollution, learn about and support local businesses who are cutting waste, and join [Reduce Your Use Tampa Bay](https://www.reduceyourusetampabay.org/){target="_blank"}!
